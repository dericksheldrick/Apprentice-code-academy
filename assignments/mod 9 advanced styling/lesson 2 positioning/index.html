CSS Positioning Examples

Static Positioning

This box is statically positioned. It's the default position, and it follows the normal document flow.

Relative Positioning

This box is relatively positioned. It's positioned relative to its normal position, moved by 10px down and 20px to the right.

Absolute Positioning

This box is absolutely positioned. It's positioned relative to its nearest positioned ancestor.

Fixed Positioning

This box is fixed. It stays in the same place even when you scroll the page.

Sticky Positioning

This box is sticky. It will stick to the top of the container as you scroll down.

Scroll down to see the sticky effect.
