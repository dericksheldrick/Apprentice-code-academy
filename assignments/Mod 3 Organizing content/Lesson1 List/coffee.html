Coffee Recipe

Below is a simple recipe for making a coffee. Get your mug Ready

shopping list

  • Coarsely ground coffee beans
  • Sugar (optional)
  • Milk or cream (optional)

Recipe

  1. Grind the Coffee Beans
    If you haven't already, grind your coffee beans to a coarse consistency. You can do this at home with a coffee grinder.
  2. Boil the Water
    Heat the water in a kettle or on the stove until it reaches just below boiling point, around 200°F (93°C)
  3. Prepare the Coffee
    If using a coffee maker, place the coffee filter in the basket and add the ground coffee. Pour the hot water into the coffee maker's reservoir and start the brewing process. If not using a coffee maker, simply add the ground coffee to a mug.
  4. Let it Brew
    Allow the coffee to brew for about 4-5 minutes. If using a coffee maker, wait for the brewing cycle to complete.
  5. Serve
    Once brewed, pour the coffee into a mug. Add sugar, milk, or cream to taste if desired
  6. Enjoy
    Stir the coffee and enjoy your freshly brewed cup
